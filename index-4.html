01 / ABOUT

Designer
by craft.
Storyteller
by instinct.

I'm Jigar Jaroli — a senior creative designer based in Udaipur, India. For the past six years I've helped restaurants, resorts, fintech apps, and government campaigns find their visual voice.

My work lives at the intersection of brand identity, motion, and editorial design. From PhonePe campaigns to luxury hospitality branding for Ramee Royal, Vatsalya Vihar, and Anandam Resort — I move fluently between static, vector, and motion.

Most recently leading international + PAN India showroom branding (Dubai included) at Heyconcrete, directing shoots, owning collateral, and shaping how brands show up in physical spaces.

Off the clock you'll find me traveling, sketching pixel art, or arguing about typography over chai.
